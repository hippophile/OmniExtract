## Why

Banks like Optima Bank Greece process thousands of documents daily — invoices, loan applications, KYC packets, statements, contracts — almost entirely by hand. OmniExtract demonstrates that a single AI-powered extraction pipeline can replace this manual effort across every document type, with no per-format configuration.
